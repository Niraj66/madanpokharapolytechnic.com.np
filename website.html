About Madanpokhara Polytechnic Institute

This is the institute located in the beautiful valley of madi. it is located in Tansen Municipality -09 palpa. the only college under the affiliation of ctevt
It was established in 2075 with the starting of Diploma in Plant Science
Madan Pokhara Polytechnic Institute is a constituent school of CTEVT and is established in 2011 A.D. It is located in Damkada in ward no.09 in Palpa district. It has been offering Diploma courses in Agriculture (Plant and Animal Science), Information Technology, and Forestry. Madanpokhara poliitechnic institute(MPPI) was established in 2018 under the Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT) as its own constituted school to provide technical education and vocational trainings related to agriculture and Infromation Technology as well as various other vocational trainings.The school is located at Tansen Municipality ward No 9,Damkada,palpa MPPI has 120 ropani of land having its own building like Admin block,Academic block(Theory and practical Buildings),Agriculture farm(Vagetable,fruit orchard,food crops,and livestock farm and fish pond) and very dense natural forest in the beginning MPPI started the Diploma in plant Science. Now we offer three diploma courses viz. Diploma in Agriculture(Plant Science),Diploma in Agriculture(Animal Science) And Diploma in Infromation Technology. Beside rugular program, we have been provided various vocational short term training. In the fiscal year 2075/76,we provided vocational training to 600 individuals.
